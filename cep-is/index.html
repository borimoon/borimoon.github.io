The Professional Cooperation of Chemical, Energetics, and Pharmaceutical Investment Service Companies

The professional cooperation of our companies offer construction and streel structure design, electromechanical and instrumental design, as well as electrical, mechanical technology, pipefitting and instrument design and engineering capacities and knowledge base for the establishment of First Generation-based starch and bioethanol plants.

The professional cooperation of the Chemical, Energetics and Pharmaceutical Investment Services consists of the following companies: Vegyterv Zrt.; LRG Kft.; Future Plastik Kft.; A&T Technology Kft.; TMK Szerviz Kft.; Slopmax Kft.; Park Air Kft.; TMK Fejlesztő Kft.; Electromatic SRL.

The professional collaboration of our companies has enabled us to offer complex technological solutions in the fields of Chemical, Energetics, Pharmaceutical and environmental technology.
This means that the business scope of the companies complement each other to offer a high quality professional service for the customers throughout the lifecycle of the provided industrial solutions.

The full life cycle services offered by our companies are the following:

  • Technical preparation for the investments
  • Development and design of unique technologies and equipment
  • Technical design, basic and implementation plans for the investments
  • Execution of investments as main contractors
  • Production of power transmission and low-voltage equipment
  • Production of technological equipment
  • Technological pipefitting
  • Professional implementation of Electrical and instrumental installations
  • Professional implementation in special explosion-proof areas
  • Installation, maintenance and repair of industrial Instruments and control systems of executing bodies
  • Demolition of obsolete industrial investments in chemical, pharmaceutical and environmental technology (planning, implementation)

Management and coordination of the activities: management of research development activities, implementation of unique developments starting from the idea until it is marketed, which requires considerable expertise, provided by systematic coordinated professional consultation of designated coordinators of the companies. The activities are carried out by high level experienced engineer colleagues.
