About us

With its dominant market share, Holofon Zrt. is one of the leading plastic recycling company of Hungary. The Company was established in 1995 by professionals with expertise acquired in plastic industry, processing and distribution. Our philosophy is to change the public approach to plastic waste. Our main activity is recycling thermoplasts having generated mainly in the form of packing waste in the field of industry and trade. The secondary raw material so processed and recycled is an excellent base material for plastic processors for manufacturing finished products.

  • buying up

    Our core activity is taking over thermoplast wastes originating from the industry, trade and households for the purpose of processing and then recycling.

  • technology

    Recycling is performed through an environment friendly technology whose basic processes are selecting, crushing, grinding and reprocessing.

  • products

    Out of mass thermoplasts, our Company has built up its production base primarily on polyolefines (PE and PP) and in a small proportion on polystyrene.

We had our system certified at the end of September 2009.
