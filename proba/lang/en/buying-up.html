Buying up

Our main activity is the purchase and recycling of thermoplastics originated from industrial, trade and household source. These materials consist mainly of packaging waste and are recycled physically.

For selling of plastic waste or purchase of regranulates, please e-mail us at info@holofon.hu

Basic requirements expected from wastes to be purchased:

  • Sorted by material type
  • Cleaned condition
  • Being free from harmful or hazardous substances

We buy up the following types of plastics

We buy up thermoplast wastes or regrinds made of HDPE, LDPE, PP or PS.

Bottle caps

Plastic regrind

We don't purchase PET or PVC wastes!

  • High and Low Density Polyethylene (HDPE, LDPE) plastic wastes

    • clear and coloured foils (except for soil covers used in agriculture)
    • cans, plastic bottles, bottle caps, plugs
    • crates, cases, barrels, tanks
    • pallets
    • pipes, plates
    • other selected moulded or extrusion-type wastes

  • Polypropylene (PP) plastic wastes

    • BOPP foils
    • trays, cases
    • woven bags
    • bottle caps, glasses, plastic bottles
    • plates, buckets
    • pallets
    • garden furniture
    • other selected moulded or extrusion-type wastes

  • High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) plastic wastes

    • trays
    • spools, discs
    • other selected moulded or extrusion-type wastes
