Mustard.
In a different way.

The goal of the project

Using by-products from the production of mustard oil, new products are developed (mustard / mustard-based sauce) made with a new environmentally friendly and consumer friendly process.

  1. oil pressing: mustard seed mustard oil mustard seed pellets
  2. oil sedimentation: unfiltered mustard oil sludge is formed at the bottom of the oil, the oil is drained from the upper part and filtered, the sludge is “extracted” from the bottom of the barrel
  3. manufacture of mustard flour: mustard seed pellets mustard flour
  4. creating emulsion: mustard seed mustard flour mustard oil mustard sludge flavorings basic mustard
  5. product manufacturing: basic mustard flavorings finished products
